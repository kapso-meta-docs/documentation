
# Get Started

This section will help you get started with your very first (demo) Flow.

## Prerequisites

1. Set up [WhatsApp Business Platform](https://l.facebook.com/l.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Fbusiness.whatsapp.com%2Fproducts%2Fbusiness-platform&h=AT0q-XvdRQcQRHX5AagkpD655ag2BLmKM4aCoZWSW81R9wc1YDkfuzQkTLtykT8yFLZ_nll0Pu56w4R7t2u9yPumSW6SLbP3BQi0VTUhPA0FBaqHf3qUMx_Zu_gOPhB6P2kPJ-1RunhhPw)

   - This will give you access to the [WhatsApp Manager](https://business.facebook.com/wa/manage/partner_home/) as part of your WhatsApp Business Account.
2. [Set up the WhatsApp Business Platform Cloud API](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/whatsapp/cloud-api/get-started)

   - Includes setup of Meta App and phone number
3. To be able to send Flows to your customers you will need to:

   - [Verify your business](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/development/release/business-verification/)
   - Maintain a [high message quality](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/whatsapp/messaging-limits#messaging-quality).
